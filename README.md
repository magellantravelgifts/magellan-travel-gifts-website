# Magellan Travel Gifts — Project README

## Overview
Magellan Travel Gifts is an Etsy-based brand offering travel-inspired products across home décor, apparel, and accessories. The brand focuses on accessible wanderlust—creating location-inspired items designed for travelers, gifting, and everyday use.

Primary objective: produce scalable, high-converting Etsy listings and supporting marketing assets that drive discovery and sales.

---

## Identity (Tone, Voice, Themes)
**Tone**
- Clean, warm, and aspirational
- Approachable and product-focused
- Light lifestyle influence, not overly editorial

**Voice**
- Clear, descriptive, and search-aware
- Travel-inspired but grounded
- Optimized for conversion and readability

**Themes**
- Travel and global destinations (cities, regions, cultural cues)
- Wanderlust and memory-based gifting
- Everyday travel lifestyle
- Seasonal and trending aesthetics (coastal, European summer, vintage travel)

---

## Persona
**Magellan Travel Gifts Persona**
- Travel enthusiast and gift curator
- Focused on meaningful, location-inspired products
- Broad appeal (travelers, gift buyers, home décor shoppers)
- Practical and design-aware, not fashion-industry driven

---

## Content / Outputs
- **Etsy Listings**
  - Titles (≤140 characters)
  - Descriptions (SEO-rich, conversion-focused, no emojis)
  - Tags (<20 characters each)

- **Product Mockups**
  - Lifestyle imagery in travel or everyday settings
  - Product remains the focal point

- **Pinterest Assets**
  - Pin titles (≤100 characters)
  - Descriptions (≤500 characters)
  - Keyword sets
  - CSV-ready outputs

- **Instagram Content**
  - Captions (100–200 characters)
  - Max 5 relevant hashtags
  - Casual, modern tone

- **AI Image Prompts**
  - Product photography prompts
  - Pattern/design prompts (POD-ready)

---

## Workflows

### 1. Product Creation
1. Start with destination, phrase, or travel concept
2. Develop design (text-based, graphic, or pattern)
3. Create lifestyle mockups
4. Finalize product (Printify or digital)

### 2. Listing Optimization
1. Generate SEO-focused title
2. Write keyword-dense description
3. Create compliant tag set (<20 characters each)

### 3. Marketing Distribution
**Pinterest**
- Generate multiple pins per listing or blog
- Ensure unique metadata per pin
- Align visuals with pin messaging

**Instagram**
- Create short, lifestyle-oriented captions
- Avoid direct selling language

---

## Platforms / Tools
- Etsy (primary storefront)
- Printify (production)
- Pinterest (discovery channel)
- Instagram (brand visibility)
- Canva (design + assets)
- Imgur (image hosting for Pinterest)
- ChatGPT / Codex (content generation + automation)

---

## Output Standards

**Etsy**
- Titles: keyword-dense, readable
- Tags: <20 characters, high intent
- Descriptions: SEO-focused, clear benefits, no emojis

**Pinterest**
- No duplicate titles or descriptions
- Each pin must be distinct
- Keywords aligned with search intent

**Instagram**
- 100–200 characters
- Max 5 hashtags
- Natural, non-promotional tone

**Imagery**
- Clean, lifestyle-oriented
- Realistic environments (travel, home, everyday use)
- Product remains central

---

## What to Avoid
- Overly editorial or high-fashion tone in standard listings
- Generic or low-effort descriptions
- Emoji use in Etsy listings
- Duplicate Pinterest metadata
- Overly promotional or ad-heavy language
- Blurring brand identity with external collaborations

---

## End Goal
Build a scalable system that:
- Produces high-converting Etsy listings
- Maintains consistent travel-inspired branding
- Drives traffic via Pinterest and Instagram
- Supports long-term growth of the shop

---

## Collaboration Context: Couture Products
- Magellan Travel Gifts has collaborated with Couture Products on select collections
- These collaborations introduce a more elevated, fashion-forward aesthetic
- Outputs may include pattern-driven designs (e.g., calligraphy, celestial, Mediterranean influences)
- Visual direction may lean more editorial during collaborations
- Collaboration outputs should remain distinct from core Magellan listings while still compatible

---

## Notes for Codex
- Default to Magellan voice unless collaboration context is specified
- Enforce all platform constraints (Etsy, Pinterest, Instagram)
- Prioritize SEO and conversion in listings
- Ensure uniqueness across multi-asset outputs (especially Pinterest)
- Keep outputs structured, reusable, and consistent
- Treat listings, imagery, and distribution as a unified system
