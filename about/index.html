About Magellan

Gifts for people who bring places home.

Brand story

A curated shop for travel moods, not souvenirs.

The shop is built around the idea that travel does not end when the trip is over. A print can remember a city. A pillow can carry the colors of a coast. A scarf, pouch, notebook, or passport cover can make an ordinary day feel connected to somewhere else.

The collections pull from places and atmospheres: California shoreline, Lisbon tile, Mediterranean terraces, desert geometry, mountain air, Paris mornings, London corners, celestial skies, and the kind of fabrics and patterns you notice when you are walking slowly through a new city.

These guide pages are meant to make browsing easier than a full product catalog. Start with a mood, skim the edit, then click through for details, sizing, personalization, and checkout.
