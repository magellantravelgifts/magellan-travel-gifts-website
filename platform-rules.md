# Platform Rules

## Etsy

- Titles must be 140 characters or fewer.
- Tags must be under 20 characters each.
- Descriptions should be SEO-rich, readable, and conversion-focused.
- No emojis in listings.

## Pinterest

- Pin titles must be 100 characters or fewer.
- Pin descriptions must be 500 characters or fewer.
- Metadata should be unique across pins.
- Keywords should match the theme and search intent, not only the product.

## Instagram

- Captions should usually be 100 to 200 characters.
- Use no more than 5 relevant hashtags.
- Keep the tone casual, modern, and non-promotional.

## Imagery

- Product remains central.
- Use realistic travel, home, or everyday lifestyle settings.
- Avoid generic mockups and duplicate-feeling visual concepts.
