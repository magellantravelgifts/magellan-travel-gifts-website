About Magellan

Inspired by the colors, cities, fabrics, and stories of travel.

Magellan Travel Gifts is built around the feeling of bringing a place home. The collections draw from cities around the world, coastal landscapes, desert light, vintage travel posters, market fabrics, tilework, color stories, and the everyday details that make a trip stay with you.

Each guide is designed for travelers, gift-givers, home decorators, and anyone who likes their everyday things to carry a little memory of elsewhere.
